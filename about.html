I am a Chicago based designer with a background in architecture and interior design. With a degree in architecture, I learned how to design in a physical space. As I transitioned from architecture to interior design, I became familiar with physical spaces on a more intimate level. This brought me to understanding the psychological experience of individual users. UX/UI Design became an interest of mine when I began creating my own online portfolio of my work. I realized how much I related to the UX world and want to explore architecture and interior design in the digital space of UX/UI Design.
